Let's get you a job.

Are you frustrated with applying for jobs and not hearing back- when you know that you are indeed qualified?

With our help, your LinkedIn profile will have an advantage over the competition.

Extraction

A member from our team uses LinkedIn to find current job listings for the job titles that you specify, and compiles a list of all the Skills required for the job.

We continue to inspect jobs until we have amassed a satisfactory list of required Skills, or until there are no more postings.

Comparison

Based on the obtained list of required Skills, we rank Skills based on how frequently they were required in job listings

Finally, we compare the extracted Skills with the Skills from your LinkedIn profile.

Reporting

The final step is to present this data back to you so that you can take the next steps to improve your profile.

This report gives you a list of the top skills that can benefit you and set your profile apart in your job hunt, as well as additional insight to help you refine your profile.

From our findings, a lot of job seekers have the required Skills for a job, but because they do not have the exact 'Skill' added on to their profile causing recruiters to overlook their application.

Sometimes, the Skill on their profile, though valid, does not match the exact keyword defined in LinkedIn's Skill list. For example: a user might have 'Illustrator' listed on their profile, while a recruiter is filtering through applicants with the defined LinkedIn skill: 'Adobe Illustrator'.

With this service, we find exactly what recruiters are looking for you to better prepare you against your competition.
