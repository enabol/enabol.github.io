Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2024

1. Introduction

Enabol ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our leads and clients. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and safeguarding of your personal information. Our operations are based in Ajax, Canada, and we comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) of Canada and adhere to the principles of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for our European clients.

2. Information Collection and Use

We collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, and business information solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiries and providing you with requested services. We do not use this information for marketing, analytics, or any other purposes.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your personal information is not shared with third-party services and is kept strictly within our company, except as required by law.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

Under PIPEDA and GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Please contact us if you wish to exercise these rights.

6. International Data Transfers

As a company operating internationally, we may transfer and store the information we collect in jurisdictions outside of where it was collected. In such cases, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place for the protection of your data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any significant changes will be communicated through our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@enabol.com.
