Get a report.

Obtaining a report is very simple. You need to give us a few specifics about your job hunt, and in return give us some time to collect the data and generate a report for you.

What we need from you

  1. A list of job titles.
  2. A list of your LinkedIn Skills.

What you will get back from us

  1. A set of reports (.pdf) for each job title.
  2. A set of lists (.csv) of Skills found for the job.

Instructions

Instructions for easily obtaining a list of your skills directly from LinkedIn can be found here.

Once ready, follow the steps below:

  1. Complete the payment below, and note the confirmation number.
  2. Send us an email at skills@enabol.com with the following information:
    • Your full name.
    • Up to 3 job titles that you would like for us to look for jobs under e.g., Mechanical Engineer, Systems Engineer, Design Engineer.
    • A .csv file attachment of the Skills from your LinkedIn profile.
    • (Optional) Location for where you want us to search for job listings. By default we use United States.
    • Include the payment Transaction ID, as well as the email address used for the payment in the email.

Feel free to add any additonal info/parameters in your email and we'll try to help to the best of our ability.

Upon receiving payment, allow us upto 1 business day to return your reports to you. However, we try to turnaround within 2-3 hours.
